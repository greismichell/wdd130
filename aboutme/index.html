About Me

Greis

I love to play with my children, spend time with my husband and them, spend time with my friends, and cook, specially made desserts for my family and friends.

My hobbies are watching movies, reading, drawing, listening to music, and learning new things.

I am so excited to learn more about programming and web design because I trust that this will helps me to improve my professional skills and develop new ones.
